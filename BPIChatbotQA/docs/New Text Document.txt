 FSC Admin Data Upload and Record Creation

GIVEN that I am an FSC Admin
WHEN I receive the CSV File from IB Reports Admin
THEN I should be able to have the access to upload the data to Salesforce
AND I should be able to confirm that the corresponding ALCO Yield and OB records are created
AND I should be able to confirm that the record type used for ALCO Yield and OB records is "Yield/OB"

IB Reports Admin Record Accessibility

GIVEN that I am an IB Reports Admin
WHEN I am informed that the ALCO Yield & OB records are already uploaded by FSC Admin
THEN I should be able to have the access to the new records created for ALCO Yields & OB records
AND I should be able to confirm that the corresponding ALCO Yield and OB fields are visible and values are stored
AND I should be able to confirm that the record type used for ALCO Yield and OB records is "Yield/OB"
AND I should be able to confirm that the page layout differs from the ALCO records created.

Post Upload Error Screen

GIVEN that I am a FSC Admin tasked to upload ALCO Yield and OB data
AND I am using Data Import Wizard when uploading the data
WHEN I encountered an error post uploading the ALCO Yield and OB data
THEN I should be able to see the job results stating the number of successfully uploaded data
AND I should be able to see the corresponding entries having errors and their causes of said errors
AND I should be receiving a notification regarding the overall status of the upload on my registered email address associated to my user record in Salesforce
